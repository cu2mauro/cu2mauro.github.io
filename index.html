Mauro Giliberti

Postdoctoral researcher · Goethe University Frankfurt

I am a theoretical physicist working at the interface of holography and relativistic astrophysics. I obtained my Ph.D. at the University of Florence with the "Strings in Florence" group, with a thesis on holographic methods for confinement. I am currently a postdoc in the group of L. Rezzolla and C. Ecker (CRC 211, Project B09), where I work on MHz gravitational waves from binary neutron star mergers, combining numerical relativity / GRMHD with holographic models of dense matter.

A complete list of my publications is available on my INSPIRE profile.
