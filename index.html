Mauro Giliberti

Postdoctoral researcher · Goethe University Frankfurt

I am a theoretical physicist working at the interface of holography and relativistic astrophysics. I obtained my Ph.D. at the University of Florence with the "Strings in Florence" group, under the supervision of Aldo L. Cotrone, with a thesis on numerical methods for holography and high-energy theoretical physics. I am currently a postdoc in the CRC-TR 211 collaboration at Goethe University Frankfurt, where I work on phase-transition dynamics in binary neutron-star mergers and on their gravitational-wave signatures.

A complete and up-to-date list of my publications is available on my INSPIRE-HEP profile and on the publications page of this site.
